    our cause

    All proceeds from this year's benefit will go towards funding the operational costs of a Special Nursery Care unit at the China Care Home in Beijing. The nursery provides round-the-clock professional care for medically fragile orphaned infants both pre and post-surgery. Since we've begun funding this nursery unit last April, the unit has provided care for over 20 orphaned babies.

    Harvard China Care is an independent, student-run organization of Harvard College that brings together a team of dedicated undergraduates determined to help build brighter futures for abandoned, orphaned, and special-needs children in China.

    Begun in 2003 by Gary Cooney '05, Harvard China Care is the first college club of the national nonprofit organization China Care Foundation. Since our founding, HCC has sent over 50 volunteers to China's orphanages and raised over $200,000 to fund surgeries, build a home for visually impaired orphans, and cover operational costs of children welfare homes in China.
