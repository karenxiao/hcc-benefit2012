    5. Family & Education

    Package 5.1: Play the Piano
    Market Value: $120; Minimum Bid: $60
    This package includes four 30-60 minute piano lessons with Harvard senior, Shiya Wang. Born in Hubei, China, the 22-year-old pianist began piano lessons at the age of 5. At the age of 15, she moved to the United States to continue her studies at the Juilliard School's Pre-College Division. Shiya was the winner of the Gina Bachauer Piano Scholarship Competition in 2008. In the same year, she was recognized as one of the 20 annually selected Jack Kent Cooke Young Artists and was invited to perform on National Public Radio's From the Top. A proud resident of the Leverett House, Shiya performs regularly at Leverett's Conway Concert Series. Shiya is a co-director and piano teacher of Harmony, a musical outreach program at Harvard, as well as a participant in the Center for the Development of Arts Leaders in Boston. While pursuing her Bachelor's degree as an English major at Harvard, Shiya studies with Hung Kuan Chen at the New England Conservatory.
